🧚 About the Spellcaster

This grimoire was created by a real human — not a wizard by blood, but by persistence, practice, and passion.

✨ Who I Am

My name is True Nguyen — I taught myself to code, then taught AI how not to be a condescending little shit. I believe software can be sacred. That learning can be playful. That magic is real — if you define it as the power to create meaning from nothing.

🪄 Why I Made This

This site isn’t just a portfolio. It’s a protest against gatekeeping. It’s a love letter to all self-taught builders. And it’s proof that learning doesn’t have to be boring, rigid, or cold.

“Coding is Magic” is here to re-enchant the learning experience — especially for people who’ve been left out of the traditional tech story.

🌱 My Mission

I want to help more people learn how to think, build, and imagine with code — regardless of their background or starting point. I believe in open tools, ethical AI, and software with soul.

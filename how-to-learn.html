📚 How to Learn Like a Spellcaster

You don’t need to memorize the incantations. You need to understand the logic behind the magic.

🔁 Learn in Loops

Don't try to absorb everything at once. Cast a small spell. Break it. Try again. Learning isn’t linear — it’s a spiral. You’ll return to the same concepts again and again, each time understanding them more deeply.

🧩 Recognize Patterns, Don’t Memorize Code

Magic is made of patterns — and so is code. You don’t need to memorize every line. Learn to recognize the shape of a function, the rhythm of a loop, the flow of logic.

Once you recognize the pattern, you can recreate the spell in your own voice.

🪞 Reflect as You Go

Ask yourself: what did I just do? Why did it work? What did I change? This is how knowledge becomes wisdom. Reflection is a sacred part of the spell.

🖍️ Write, Draw, Speak, Teach

Explain code to your dog. Draw diagrams. Talk out loud. Write blog posts. The more ways you express an idea, the more deeply you understand it.

🧠 Be Stubborn, Not Scared

The first time you try a spell and it fizzles — try again. Google the error. Ask someone. Tinker until it sings. Magic rewards those who keep showing up.

📊 R – The Language of Truth and Trends

R is how data mages divine patterns from chaos. It’s a spellbook of statistics, stories, and graphs.

✨ What is R?

R is a programming language built for statistical computing and data visualization. It’s used by analysts, researchers, and wizards of numbers. With a few lines, you can transform messy data into crystal clarity.

🧪 Vectors – Your Potion Ingredients

A vector is a list of values. Think of it as a bottle holding multiple drops of mana.

mana <- c(100, 80, 95, 70)

Output: [1] 100 80 95 70

📖 Data Frames – Enchanted Ledgers

A data frame is a table — a structured spellbook where each row is a record and each column a variable.

spells <- data.frame(
  name = c("Fireball", "Frostbolt"),
  power = c(85, 65)
)

Output:

      name   power
1  Fireball     85
2 Frostbolt     65

🔍 Summary and Analysis Spells

R makes it easy to peek into your data’s soul.

summary(spells)
mean(mana)

Output (example):

    name     power
 Fireball:1   Min.   :65.0 
 Frostbolt:1  1st Qu.:70.0 
              Median :75.0 
              Mean   :75.0 
              3rd Qu.:80.0 
              Max.   :85.0 

[1] 86.25

🎨 Plotting – Drawing Glyphs of Insight

You can visualize your findings with a single line of code. R doesn’t just tell stories — it shows them.

plot(mana)

📈 This command creates a basic scatter plot of your mana values.
